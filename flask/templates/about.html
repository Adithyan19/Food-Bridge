About Us

Who We Are

Food Bridge is a community-driven initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and helping those in need. Our volunteers actively participate in collecting surplus food and delivering it to shelters and communities facing food insecurity. Our mission is to bridge the gap between excess and necessity, ensuring that food reaches those who need it the most.

Our Mission

At Food Bridge, we believe that no one should go hungry while food goes to waste. We collaborate with restaurants, grocery stores, and individuals to redistribute surplus food efficiently and sustainably.
